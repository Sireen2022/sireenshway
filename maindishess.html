Brosted


1_In a large shallow dish,combine 3 cups flour,garlic salt ,paprika,2 teaspoons pepper and teaspoons poultry seasoning. 2_ in another shallow dish ,beat eggs and 2 cups water ,add 1 teaspoon salt and the remaining 1 cup flour and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.

3-Dip chicken in egg mixture , then place in flour mixture ,a few pieces at a time.turn to coat.

4-In a deep-fat fryer ,heat oil to 375 degree .

5_Working in batches ,fry chicken several pieces at a time until skin is golden brown about 7-8 minutes.

6_Drain on paper towels.
